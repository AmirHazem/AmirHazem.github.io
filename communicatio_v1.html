Courses

Our language courses offer a unique and dynamic learning experience that combines the best of different worlds: blended, face-to-face and e-learning programs. We have meticulously designed our courses to cater to the diverse needs and preferences of our clients, while ensuring flexibility for everyone.

Legal English

This specialised legal English course aims to develop learners’ ability to  master key legal terminology and interpret legal documents, while strengthening understanding of  the Common Law  system. The live sessions focus on participating effectively in professional interactions such as negotiations and client consultations.

Read More

Business English

Participants strengthen their ability to communicate effectively in professional and corporate settings. The focus is on meetings, presentations, negotiations, and clear written communication.  Learners develop strategic vocabulary and an appropriate professional tone. Attention is also given to intercultural awareness in international business environments.

Read More

English for Tourism

Designed for service-oriented roles, this programme focuses on practical communication skills for the tourism sector.  Learners acquire strategies to interact with visitors in real-life situations such as bookings, tours, and customer support. With the emphasis  on clarity, politeness, and cultural sensitivity,  the objective is to ensure confident and professional communication in a global tourism context.

How it Works

  1. Check our list of available courses

    Browse our specialised courses by topic, level, or industry. Each includes objectives, duration, and outcomes so you know exactly what you’re signing up for. Getting started is simple and intuitive. Our platform is designed to guide you step by step, so you can focus on what really matters: learning and progressing with confidence. From registration to your first lesson, everything is clear, fast, and accessible.

  2. Test your English level

    Before signing up for a module, you are first asked to complete a short multiple choice questions test to gauge your linguistic competencies. After completing the enquiry form, you will be asked to take a free 20 minute test to evaluate your language competency.





    We know that people are not machines and each individual has their own appoach to learning. That is why we feel it is essential to contact you personally to draw up a personalised learning trajectory together.

  3. Choose your Plan

    Select the subscription that best fits your goals, schedule, and learning pace. Choose from online, blended and face to face. Whether you are looking for self-study resources, guided courses, or live sessions with experts, each plan gives you access to high-quality content and practical tools tailored to your needs. Change or upgrade anytime.

  4. Log in and start your learning journey

    Once registered, log in to access your personalised learning space. Track your progress, complete interactive lessons, join live sessions, and revisit resources whenever you need. Learn at your own pace, anywhere, anytime, and take steady steps toward your personal or professional goals.
